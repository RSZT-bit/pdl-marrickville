Private Coaching — Coach Rents Court Model

How it works

Private coaches hire a court at a discounted rate, then charge their clients directly at whatever rate they like. PDL is the venue, not the employer. Zero coaching staff cost to PDL. Zero admin overhead. Court earns rent from an otherwise empty slot.

Coach court rates

Peak (4pm–10pm + weekends): $96/1.5hr (20% off $120)
Off-peak (9am–4pm weekdays): $71/1.5hr (20% off $89)
Early (6am–9am): $62/1.5hr (20% off $78)

Coach bills client separately — PDL just collects the court rent.

Volume target

6–10 private coaches on the roster. Each runs 15–25 hrs/week. ~150 coach-court hours/week.

At blended $80 rent × 150 hrs × 50 weeks = $600K/yr coaching revenue — off-peak hours that would otherwise be empty. Pure court utilisation gain.

Launch Mechanics — premium & limited, no discounts

1. Waitlist Before Day 1

Applications open 90 days before launch. No pricing shown publicly — applicants see it after submitting interest. Creates scarcity. Target: 1,000+ applications before opening.

2. Founding Wall

First 200 Owner-tier members get their name engraved on the Founding Wall, locker #1–200 reserved for life, and a lifetime rate lock (never raised). No discount — status and permanence.

3. Owner Tier Invitation-Only

After initial 200, Owner tier becomes invitation-only. Application + interview. Ensures the room stays premium. Equinox, Soho House, The Wing all gate this way.

4. No Discounts Ever

No Groupon. No launch sale. No "first month free." Pricing holds from Day 1 — this is the premium signal. Discount once, and you're a gym forever.

5. Pro League → Content

Pro League matches filmed weekly. Pro + Owner members become the brand's public face. Free marketing. Built-in audience for ticketed tournament events on pro courts.

6. Annual Billing Option

Pay 12 months upfront, get 1 month free (tier-dependent). Locks revenue, reduces churn admin. Especially important for Pro and Owner tier cash flow certainty.
